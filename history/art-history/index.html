Art History



Another perspective on world events, Art History is the examination of history through the arts.



  • Smarthistory — This website has a variety of articles and videos that help you learn about the various complexities and features of artwork throughout world history. Smarthistory also lets users access a variety of additional, specific resources.
  • The Heilbrunn Timeline of Art History — The Metropolitan Museum of Art's Heilbrunn Timeline of Art History can give you context, through artwork, of the civilizations of the era.
