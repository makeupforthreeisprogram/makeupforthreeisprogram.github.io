History in the Mediterranean



The Mediterranean was the link between many of the most well-known civilizations of the ancient world — ancient Rome, Greece, and Egypt among them — but many others too.



  • Ancient Egypt from History.com — This resource gives a good overview and timeline of the various periods in Egyptian history, from the predynastic to Hellenistic eras. Ancient Egyptian history can seem complex, with the Old, Middle, and New Kingdoms, but this resource simplifies it.
  • Ancient Egypt from the Ancient History Encyclopedia — An alternative to the History.com resource, the Ancient History Encyclopedia as a whole is an excellent source.
  • Ancient Greece, from the Encyclopaedia Britannica — A general overview of the civilization, this comprehensive timeline is vital to understanding the Ancient Greeks. The page has dozens of sections, from the causes of the Persian Wars to the administration of democracy.
  • Khan Academy's introduction to Etruria — Read this short article for an overview of the predecessors to the Romans, the civilization you never knew about.
